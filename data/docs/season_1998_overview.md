# 1998 MLB Season Overview

This assistant’s SQL warehouse contains 1998 season statistics for MLB players and teams.

## What this dataset covers

- Regular-season player and team stats for 1998.
- Team records and division standings.
- Awards and All-Star roster info (where available).
- Advanced FanGraphs metrics (WAR, wOBA, wRC+, FIP) for 1998.

## Useful questions to ask

- “Who hit the most home runs in 1998?”
- “What was the Yankees’ regular-season record in 1998?”
- “Who had the best ERA in 1998 (minimum innings pitched)?”
- “Who led MLB in WAR in 1998?”
- “Which teams were in the AL East in 1998, and what were their records?”

## Notes

- This dataset is historical. It does not include live scores or current-season data.
- Pitch-level Statcast data does not exist for 1998; this warehouse focuses on season-level aggregates.
